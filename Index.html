Everything Everywhere All At Ocnce

My name is Brenda, I was born on a warm, sunny day in January in Nicaragua. Maybe it was warm and sunny - who knows. My parents were Bob and Ines, they are both deceased. I grew up in Florida with my aunt and cousin. Things were different back then and time seemed to go slower. I joined the military right out of highschool becasue we didn't have much money to pay for school. I retired out of the military early due to an injury that couldn't be fixed. I've deployed many times and seen and learned about so many different cultures.
